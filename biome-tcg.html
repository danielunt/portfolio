Old Logo

Biome spans every habitat and ecosystem on Earth, making it challenging to find a single symbol that represents them all. However, by focusing on something universally present, such as the Sun, I found a natural and unifying element to build their logo around.

New Logo

The previous logo had a nice look, but it lacked recognizability. A simple yellow, paintbrush-style circle just was not enough. So, I set out to create a more distinctive symbol, one that sticks with you after just one glance. The yellow color and the original font have been kept. The yellow helps us stand out from competitors, while the font maintains a friendly, child-appropriate tone that suits a trading card game.

Biome Types

Card Layout

Layout design is crucial in trading card games, it ensures clarity, enhances gameplay, and brings the card's artwork and mechanics together in a cohesive visual experience. A well designed card is not just visually appealing, it communicates critical information with ease and through that it permits immersion.

A 3mm black border is essential, as it significantly simplifies the printing process.
